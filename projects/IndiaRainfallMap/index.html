India’s Monthly Rainfall Across Subdivisions (1901–2017)

Sources: India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India — via data.gov.in

This visualization shows how rainfall patterns have varied across India’s meteorological subdivisions from 1901–2017. Each fern represents a subdivision, with density and color reflecting monthly rainfall amounts.
