Active Sonar Simulation

A Unity simulation of an active searchlight sonar, modeled after real systems that transmit a narrow acoustic beam, switch to receive mode to capture echoes, and rotate to scan the environment. While not a fully polished simulator, it captures the core mechanics of the system without the extra complexity that would be required for a production-level model.
